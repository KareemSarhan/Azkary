Summary:Islamic remembrance (Azkar) app with prayer times and progress tracking
Description:
Azkary is a beautiful, privacy-focused Islamic remembrance (Azkar) app that helps Muslims maintain their daily dhikr routine with prayer times and intelligent progress tracking.

'''Key Features:'''

* Three Azkar Categories: Morning (Fajr-Asr), Evening (Asr-Isha), and Sleep (Isha-Fajr)
* Authentic Content: Each dhikr includes Arabic text, transliteration, translation, and hadith references
* Prayer Times: Automatic calculation based on your location with offline support
* Progress Tracking: Visual indicators and daily completion statistics
* Custom Categories: Create and organize your own dhikr collections
* Beautiful UI: Material Design 3 with dark mode and OLED support
* Multi-Language: Full English and Arabic interface support

'''Privacy First:'''

Azkary is completely offline-first and privacy-focused:
* No data collection or tracking
* No advertisements
* No internet required after initial setup
* All data stored locally on your device
